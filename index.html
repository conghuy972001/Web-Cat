~ About Us ~

Why choose us

Cat cafés benefit both customers and animals. The opportunity to have breakfast or to spend time over coffee with cats purring is very relaxing. Petting cats and listening to their purring is a real stress reliever. In addition, cat cafés are quiet and not very crowded places. For this reason, they are often chosen as study rooms by young students.

The cats we meet are mostly rescued cats looking for a home or coming from catteries, so they are more than happy to give you attention and cuddles. Neko cafés, therefore, contribute to the reduction of straying.
